Non-bonded functionsÂ¶

While the bonded components mainly resposible to model the molecular structures, the inter-molecular functions are mainly responsible to model the overall behaviour of the system model such as densities, molar volumes, heats of vapourisation, etc.

Non-bonded functions are mainly composed of two components: the short-ranged van der Waals (vdw) interactions and the long-ranged electrostatic interactions. These energy components are calculated based on interactions between pairs of atoms and the functions depend only on the interatomic distance.

Note

For atoms within a molecule, non-bonded interactions will apply on the 1-4 atoms (atom pairs that are seprated by three successive bonds) onwards.
