Exercises¶

Below is a list of tutorial exercises that have been written for a range of users of DL_Software. They are categorized into 3 levels of ‘Difficulty’: Low, Medium and High. They are written as exercises rather than walkthroughs and require input from you.

  • Low Difficulty Exercises
    • Fast Ion Conduction
    • Potassium Capture by Valinomycin
    • MD Algorithms and the Properties of Water
  • Medium Difficulty Exercises
    • Anisotropic Constant Pressure MD
    • The Potential of Mean Force for H2O-K+
    • Diffusion in Zeolites
    • Structure and Dynamics of Argon
    • Adsorption of Formic Acid onto a Calcite Surface
  • High Difficulty Exercises
    • A Carbon Nanoswitch
