DL_POLY¶

Below is a list of tutorial exercises that have been written for a range of users of DL_POLY. They are categorized into 3 levels of ‘Difficulty’: Low, Medium and High. They are written as exercises rather than walkthroughs and require input from you.

  • Low Difficulty Exercises
    • Fast Ion Conduction
    • Potassium Capture by Valinomycin
    • MD Algorithms and the Properties of Water
  • Medium Difficulty Exercises
    • Anisotropic constant pressure MD
    • The Potential of Mean Force for \(H_2O-K^+\)
    • Diffusion in Zeolites
    • Structure and Dynamics of Argon
    • Absorption of formic acid onto a calcite surface
    • Parameterising DPD interactions using Flory-Huggins solution theory
    • Lipid bilayers and micelles with DPD
  • High Difficulty Exercises
    • A Carbon Nanoswitch
