DL_F Notation and DANAIÂ¶

Central to the unique capability of D_ATA to study atomic interactions are the implementation of DL_F Notation and DANAI. These features are implemented in DL_FIELD and DL_ANALYSER, respectively.


For information about DL_F Notation, see The DL_F Notation 2 in DL_FIELD Resource Guide section.

For more information about DANAI, see Atomic Interaction Analysis and Interaction Notation - DANAI in DL_ANALYSER Resource Guide section.
