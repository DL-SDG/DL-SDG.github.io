Welcome to the DL_MESO Reference Guide main portal. If you are new to DL_MESO or you wish to refresh your memory, you have come to the right place. The contents have been extracted from DL_MESO lecture slides given in DL_Software training workshops, but modified for an online self-learning experience. 

The tutorial is split into two parts: Lattice Boltzmann Equation (LBE) and Dissipative Particle Dynamics (DPD).
