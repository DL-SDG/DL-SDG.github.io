.. This lists a few text editors and provides links to them

************
Text Editors
************

To open and manipulate input files requires `a text editor <http://www.text-editor.org/>`_ such as:

* `Emacs <https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/>`_
* `Vim <https://www.vim.org/>`_ 
* `Nano <https://www.howtogeek.com/howto/42980/the-beginners-guide-to-nano-the-linux-command-line-text-editor/>`_.
