*****************************
DL_F Notation and DANAI
*****************************

Central to the unique capability of D_ATA to study atomic interactions are the implementation of DL_F Notation and DANAI. These features are implemented in DL_FIELD and DL_ANALYSER, respectively.

|br|

For information about DL_F Notation, see :doc:`dlf_10` in DL_FIELD Resource Guide section.
|br| |br|
For more information about DANAI, see :doc:`dla_8` and :doc:`dla_9` in DL_ANALYSER Resource Guide section.
