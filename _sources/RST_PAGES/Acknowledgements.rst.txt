.. This page lists acknowledgements of the  contributors of material to this resource centre

  
****************
Acknowledgements
****************

We would like to thank all contributors to the DL_Software Digital Guide, and hope that should you not find yourself individually listed below, you will assume our appreciation of your contribution - thank you!

If you have any comments, suggestions, or anything to contribute, please email me (Chin Yong).

* `Chin Yong <https://www.scd.stfc.ac.uk/Pages/Chin-Yong.aspx>`_ Originator of DL_SDG concept, author and compositor of the Digital Guide and design consultant. Main contributor to DL_FIELD, D_ATA and DL_ANALYSER contents.
  
* Dawn Geatches. Author and compositor, who had designed the original framework for DL_SDG.

* `Ilian Todorov <https://www.scd.stfc.ac.uk/Pages/Ilian-Todorov.aspx>`_ Originator of DL_SDG concept.
 
* `Michael Seaton <https://www.scd.stfc.ac.uk/Pages/Michael-Seaton.aspx>`_ Main contributor to DL_MESO and DL_POLY content.

* `Alin Elena <https://www.scd.stfc.ac.uk/Pages/Alin-Elena.aspx>`_ Main contributor of Advanced Installation details

* `Andrey Brukhno <https://www.scd.stfc.ac.uk/Pages/Andrey-Brukhno.aspx>`_ Main contributor of advice on how to get a parallel version of DL_POLY up-and-running on HPC    

* Ashraf Hussain - Lead programmer for Cu Impact Visualiser.

* Zakaria Loizou - Converts Democritus from Java to Python and collates materials information.
  
* George Roe - Converts Democritus from Python to Javascripts and deploy in DL_SDG. 

* `Linux Cheat Sheet <https://www.linuxtrainingacademy.com/linux-commands-cheat-sheet/#1_8211_SYSTEM_INFORMATION>`_ provided most of the Linux commands listed on the cheat sheet  

Provision of fundings to make DL_SDG possible are greatly appreciated, in particular the Ada Lovelace Centre, a digital asset development centre in support of `UK national large-scale facilities <https://stfc.ukri.org/research/our-science-facilities/>`_.
